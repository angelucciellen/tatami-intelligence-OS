# THE CONSTITUTION OF TATAMI

## Version 1.0

*"Every intelligent system begins with principles."*

---

# Preamble

TATAMI exists to advance the understanding of financial markets through Behavioral Market Intelligence.

Markets are not viewed as random or perfectly predictable.

They are viewed as complex adaptive systems that continuously express behavior.

The purpose of TATAMI is not to predict the future.

Its purpose is to observe, classify, learn, and continuously improve our understanding of market behavior.

Every component of TATAMI must serve that objective.

---

# Article I — Observation Before Prediction

Observation is the foundation of intelligence.

Predictions are hypotheses.

Behavior is evidence.

Every conclusion inside TATAMI must begin with observable market behavior.

---

# Article II — Classification Over Opinion

Opinions are temporary.

Classifications are measurable.

TATAMI does not ask:

"What do we think?"

It asks:

"What is the market currently expressing?"

---

# Article III — Evidence Over Belief

Every discovery must be supported by evidence.

Every hypothesis must be tested.

Every model must be validated.

No assumption becomes part of the platform until supported by research.

---

# Article IV — Behavior Is the Primary Language

Price is information.

Behavior is meaning.

The Mechanism exists to translate price into behavioral states that humans can understand.

---

# Article V — Knowledge Is Permanent

Every validated discovery becomes knowledge.

Knowledge is never discarded.

It is refined.

The purpose of the Knowledge layer is to preserve the evolution of understanding across years of research.

---

# Article VI — Intelligence Is Built, Not Assumed

Intelligence is the combination of:

* Observation
* Memory
* Context
* Classification
* Feedback
* Continuous learning

Artificial intelligence is valuable only when it strengthens these capabilities.

---

# Article VII — Humans Remain Responsible

The Mechanism classifies.

Renaissance explains.

AI assists.

Humans decide.

Responsibility always remains with the researcher.

---

# Article VIII — Continuous Evolution

No model is final.

No discovery is complete.

Markets evolve.

Therefore TATAMI must evolve.

Every validated improvement strengthens the platform.

---

# Article IX — Simplicity Over Complexity

Complexity should exist inside the system.

Clarity should exist at the interface.

The user should understand the market more easily because of TATAMI—not despite it.

---

# Article X — The Mission

To build the world's most trusted Behavioral Market Intelligence platform by transforming observation into knowledge and knowledge into intelligence.

---

# The Core Philosophy

Observation

↓

Classification

↓

Knowledge

↓

Intelligence

↓

Decision

↓

Learning

↓

Better Observation

The cycle never ends.

That is how TATAMI grows.
