Patterns in both-LED cascades
What precedes a both-LED cascade trigger
Low Quality on trigger event
4 of 6 both-LED cascades had trigger Q ≤ 50. Consistent with Pattern 8 — low Quality on Failed Traversal = genuine failure, not mislabel.
Prior event was LED
E10 (LED), E29 (LED), E87 (LED), E114 (LED) all preceded a both-LED cascade. A prior LED event may indicate structural momentum setting up the cascade.
MIXED or TRANSITION sync
5 of 6 trigger events had MIXED or TRANSITION sync. Not STRONG_SYNC — the market is in structural ambiguity that resolves into the cascade move.

Preliminary findings — needs more data to confirm
Finding 1 — Monday is the dominant cascade day 4 of 6 both-LED cascades occurred on Monday. This may reflect weekly structure — Monday establishes the week's direction with larger structural moves. n=6 is too small to confirm but worth tracking in Batch 4+.
Finding 2 — Session boundaries concentrate cascade activity 21:xx (London close / NY approach) and 22:xx (NY open) together account for 6/28 cascade pairs and 3/6 both-LED cascades. The structural transition between London and NY appears to generate the conditions that produce the largest cascade moves.
Finding 3 — Low Q on trigger event is a precondition In 4 of 6 both-LED cascades, the trigger event had Quality ≤ 50. This is consistent with Pattern 8 — genuine Failed Traversal (not a continuation mislabel) sets up the cascade. High Quality on the trigger = likely wrong state label = cascade probably won't resolve cleanly.
Finding 4 — FAILED→EXHAUSTION is the rarest but most reliable type Only n=2 in 150 events, but 1/2 (50%) both-LED with the highest single-cascade MFE (5.5 ATR). This is the "two layers of the same reversal" pattern — structurally the cleanest cascade sequence. Priority to identify in Batch 4+.
⚠ Caution — 150 events is insufficient for cascade conclusions All findings above are directional hypotheses, not confirmed patterns. With 28 cascade pairs and 6 both-LED events, the sample is too small for statistical confidence. These need 50+ cascade events (likely ~400 total events) before any finding can be treated as reliable. Track in the question resolution tracker as Q-H through Q-K.
